We Empower Students for Climate Action

Our climate activist group, “Climel,” exists to raise awareness, inspire action, and drive positive change in the face of the climate crisis. We believe that every student has a role to play in safeguarding our planet for future generations.

Remember, every small action counts. Whether it’s reducing plastic use, planting trees, or advocating for policy changes, students play a vital role in creating a sustainable future. Join us, and let’s be the change-makers our planet needs!

Our Purpose

Education and Awareness

We aim to educate students about the urgent environmental challenges we face—rising temperatures, deforestation, pollution, and loss of biodiversity.

Why Students Get Involved?

Impactful Youth Voice

Students have a unique perspective and energy. When we unite, our collective voice becomes a force for change.
By participating, students contribute to shaping policies, influencing public opinion, and driving real-world impact.

Learning Beyond the Classroom

Climate action isn’t just theoretical; it’s hands-on learning. Students gain practical skills—organizing events, researching, and communicating effectively.
Being part of Green Warriors enhances classroom education with real-world experiences.

Personal Growth and Leadership

Joining our group fosters leadership skills, teamwork, and resilience.
Students learn to navigate challenges, collaborate, and lead initiatives—essential skills for any future career.

Hope and Agency

Climate change can feel overwhelming, but activism provides hope. Students realize they can be part of solutions.
Taking action empowers us—it’s a way to combat eco-anxiety and channel our passion into positive change.
